CONNECTING BRANCHED MINDS

EasySIM® is a KPI-driven comprehensive Simulated Learning and Performance Cloud (SLPC) designed to disrupt the norm of plateaued job performances at your organization.
Its branched decision tree model puts your employees behind the wheel and lets them carve out a learning path of their own. It allows them to practice performing in real-life work scenarios, and its proprietary multi-competency mapping algorithm provides deep analysis of the employee's knowledge and behavior and the optimum utilization of their competencies.
